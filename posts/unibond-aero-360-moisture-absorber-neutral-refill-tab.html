Product Options: UniBond AERO 360° Moisture Absorber Neutral Refill Tab vs. Dehumidifiers

Dehumidifiers and passive dehumidifiers, like the UniBond AERO 360° Moisture Absorber Neutral Refill Tab, serve the same purpose – to remove excess moisture from the air. However, they have different operating mechanisms:

Dehumidifiers use electricity to power a fan and condenser coils, drawing in air and releasing it back into the atmosphere, now dry. Passive dehumidifiers, on the other hand, rely on chemical reactions to absorb moisture from the air.

Dehumidifiers are ideal for areas with extremely high humidity levels, such as basements or crawlspaces. Passive dehumidifiers, like the UniBond AERO 360° Moisture Absorber Neutral Refill Tab, are perfect for smaller spaces, closets, or areas with lower humidity levels.
