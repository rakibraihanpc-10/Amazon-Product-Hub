Introduction to Noise Cancellation

So, what exactly is noise cancellation, and how does it work? Honestly, it's pretty simple - noise-cancelling headphones use one or more microphones to capture ambient noise, and then produce a "negative" sound wave to cancel it out. This results in a quieter, more peaceful listening experience that's perfect for commuters, travelers, or anyone who wants to focus on their music. In my experience, the Bose QuietComfort Ultra does an exceptional job of blocking out background noise, from the hum of the engine on a plane to the chatter of strangers on a busy street.
