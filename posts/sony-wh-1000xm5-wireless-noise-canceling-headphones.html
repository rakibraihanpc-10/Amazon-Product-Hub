Introduction to Noise Canceling Headphones

Honestly, I didn't know much about noise canceling headphones before I started researching the WH-1000XM5. But it turns out, they're actually pretty interesting. Noise canceling headphones use something called active noise control to reduce unwanted ambient sounds. They do this by using one or more microphones to capture ambient noise, and then producing a "negative" sound wave to cancel it out. It's pretty cool, and it really works.

Look, I know what you're thinking - do I really need noise canceling headphones? And the answer is, it depends. If you're someone who listens to music or podcasts a lot, especially in noisy environments, then yes, noise canceling headphones are definitely worth considering. They can really make a big difference in your listening experience. But if you're just going to be using them at home or in a quiet office, then you might not need them as much.
