Key Features to Consider

One of the most important things to consider when buying noise-canceling headphones is the type of noise cancellation they use. There are two main types: active and passive. Active noise cancellation uses a microphone to pick up outside noise and then produces a "negative" sound wave to cancel it out. Passive noise cancellation, on the other hand, uses physical barriers (like ear cups or headphones) to block out outside noise. The Sony WH-1000XM5 uses active noise cancellation, which is generally more effective than passive noise cancellation.

Another thing to consider is the battery life of the headphones. If you're going to be using them for long periods of time, you'll want to make sure they have a long battery life.
