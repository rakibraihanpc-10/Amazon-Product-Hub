What is Descaling, and Why Do I Need the De'Longhi EcoDecalk Descaler DLSC500?

Descaling, or the removal of limescale buildup, is an essential part of maintaining your appliances. Limescale can cause corrosion, inefficiency, and even safety hazards in extreme cases. In my experience, regular descaling has made a significant difference in the performance of my appliances.

Limescale Buildup: Why You Need to Descale

Limescale is a naturally occurring mineral deposit that can form when hard water is heated. It's especially prevalent in areas with high water hardness. When limescale builds up in your appliances, it can lead to:

  • Reduced performance: Limescale can clog up pipes, reduce water flow, and even cause your appliances to shut down.
  • Increased energy bills: As your appliances work harder to compensate for limescale buildup, your energy bills will rise.
  • Safety risks: Excessive limescale buildup can cause appliances to overheat, leading to potential fires.
