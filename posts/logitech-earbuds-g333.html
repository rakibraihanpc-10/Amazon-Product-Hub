Pros & Cons

  • Impressive Sound Quality: The Logitech Earbuds G333 deliver impressive sound quality considering their price point. With deep, rich bass and clear, crisp highs, they're perfect for listening to music or watching videos.
  • Comfortable Fit: I was pleasantly surprised by how comfortable these earbuds are to wear. They come with three different sizes of ear tips to ensure a secure fit, and they never felt too heavy or bulky in my ears.
  • Long Battery Life: The G333 boasts an impressive battery life – up to 8 hours on a single charge, which is plenty for a long day of running errands or working out.
  • Easy Controls: The earbuds come with intuitive controls that make it easy to manage your music playback, adjust the volume, and even activate Siri or Google Assistant.
  • Stylish Design: The G333 has a sleek, modern design that looks great in any setting. They come in a range of colors to match your personal style, and the charging case is compact and easy to carry around.
  • Microphonewhile call quality could be better: While the earbuds perform well for music and video playback, the microphone quality could be improved. I experienced some distortion and audio lag during phone calls.
  • Some Lag with Gaming: I encountered some lag and delay when using the earbuds for online gaming, which was disappointing considering the price point.
  • No Water Resistance: Unfortunately, the G333 lacks water resistance, which makes them less suitable for swimming or working out in wet conditions.
