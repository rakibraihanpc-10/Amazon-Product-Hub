Logitech G333 FAQ

What kind of connectivity does the G333 offer?

The Logitech G333 supports Bluetooth 5.0 and has a convenient wireless range of up to 30 feet.

Can I use these earbuds for video calls?

Unfortunately, the in-built microphone might not be the most reliable for video conferencing due to sound quality limitations.

How do I control volume or skip tracks?

The earbuds feature intuitive in-line controls that let you easily adjust volume, switch tracks, or pause playback.

Are the G333 earbuds water-resistant?

While they're not waterproof, the earbuds have a limited water-resistance coating that should protect against minor splashes or sweat.

What else comes with the purchase?

Besides the earbuds, you'll also get a compact charging case and an additional set of ear tips for a customized fit.
