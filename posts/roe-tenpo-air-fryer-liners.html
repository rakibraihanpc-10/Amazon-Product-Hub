What to Look for in an Air Fryer Liner

Truth be told, not all air fryer liners are created equal. When shopping for a liner, here are a few things to look out for:

  • Material: Look for liners made from high-quality, non-toxic materials that won't leach chemicals into your food.
  • Thickness: Choose a liner that's not too thin or too thick. You want it to be thick enough to withstand heat and food residue but not so thick that it's difficult to clean.
  • Size: Make sure the liner fits your air fryer model. You don't want to waste money on a liner that's too large or too small.
