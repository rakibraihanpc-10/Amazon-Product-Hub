Comprehensive Buyer's Guide

If you're considering purchasing the Amazon Basics Vacuum Compression Zipper Storage Bags, here are some things to keep in mind.

Choosing the Right Size

The bags come in various sizes, ranging from 12 inches to 30 inches. It's essential to choose the right size for your needs. I ended up with the 24-inch bag, which has been perfect for my garage.

When selecting the size, consider the items you plan to store and the space constraints of your storage area. Make sure to choose a bag that's large enough to accommodate all your gear, but not so large that it takes up too much space.

Using the Vacuum Pump

Using the vacuum pump is straightforward – simply attach the hose to the bag, turn it on, and watch as the bag compresses. I was a bit skeptical at first, but the pump works incredibly well.

It's essential to note that the pump can be a bit loud, so if you plan to use it in a quiet area, you may want to consider using it during the day or when you're not at home.

Storage and Organization

The bags are designed to help you organize your gear and keep it out of the way. I use mine to store tools, equipment, and other items that take up too much space in my garage.

When storing the bags, make sure to keep them clean and dry. I also like to label them so I can easily identify what's inside each bag.
