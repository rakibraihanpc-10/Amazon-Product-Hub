Why Vacuum Compression?

Vacuum compression is an innovative technology that uses a combination of vacuum bags and a manual pump to remove air from a bag, allowing you to compress bulky items into a fraction of their original size. This technology has been around for a while, but I was still impressed by how effectively it worked – even on the biggest, bulkiest items.
